# Code Of Conduct

BarqDB should be a useful and respectful place to work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but fix harm when it happens.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment or threats
- Personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Reporting

If something feels wrong, contact a maintainer privately. Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block users when needed.
